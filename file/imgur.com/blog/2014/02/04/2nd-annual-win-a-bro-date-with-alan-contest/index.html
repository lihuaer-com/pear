2nd Annual Win a Bro Date with Alan Contest!

Don’t be forever alone this Valentine’s Day. Hang out with the Imgur team and play video games instead! Imgur loves you, and nothing says, “I love you” like free stuff. Want to win it? All you have to do is show us you care in the form of a valentine.

Here’s how it works:
Create a Valentine’s Day card for Alan Schaaf, the Founder and CEO of Imgur.
Must be:
-An original creation made by you (don’t be that guy who steals someone else’s content)
-Submitted to the Win a Bro Date Gallery (imgur.com/g/brodate) by 11:59PM PST on February 14, 2014
-Adhering to the Contest Rules (below)


credit: indigowolf

Need ideas? Check out last year’s entries. If you attempt to steal last year’s entries, we will look for you, we will find you, and we will kill you.

GRAND PRIZE:

  • 1 Bro Date with Alan Schaaf (Founder/CEO of Imgur) Jake, Carlos, Bryn, and as many of your friends or fellow Imgurians that the game supports.
    —Bro date consists of one night of online gaming with the Imgur staff on the game of your choice on the night of your choice. Standard trash talk allowed.
  • $150 store credit to the Imgur store
  • Lifetime Pro Account
  • Your Valentine’s Day card featured in the Most Viral Gallery (yes, you will keep all the reputation points you earn from this contest)

1st RUNNER UP:

  • $100 store credit to the Imgur store
  • 1 Year Pro Account

2nd RUNNER UP:

  • $50 store credit to the Imgur store
  • 1 Year Pro Account

RULES AND FINE PRINT
By entering the Win a Bro Date with Alan Contest, you agree to the following Rules.

CONTEST PERIOD: Contest begins on February 4, 2014 at 12PM PST and ends on February 14th, 2014 at 11:59PM PST.

HOW TO ENTER: NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. To enter the Contest, visit the gallery located at imgur.com/g/brodate during the contest period and upload your image directly into that gallery. Make sure you are on the Bro Date Gallery page while uploading. Select “add to gallery” and ensure the message “you are adding _ image(s) to the win a bro date gallery” appears. Help on uploading an image can be found here: imgur.com/help. Your entry is accepted when it appears on the Win a Bro Date Gallery.

VALENTINE REQUIREMENTS:
(a) Submissions are subject to all normal gallery rules and terms of service. Imgur reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any Entrant who submits a valentine not complying with these site wide regulations.

(b) Entries must be original, unpublished works.

(c) In the form of PNG, JPG or GIF.

(d) Only one entry per user will be accepted.

JUDGING: Each Valentine will be judged by a member of the Imgur staff on creativity, originality, and adherence to the theme of the contest. Score and comments by users will be taken into account, but winners will be chosen solely by Imgur.

ELIGIBILITY: Contest is open to any account holder on Imgur. If you’re under 18, ask your parents for permission before entering this contest or playing video games with Imgurians.

Email sarah@imgur.com with any questions!

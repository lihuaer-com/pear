Win a Bro Date with Alan Contest Champions

Thank you to our lovely users who took time to create a valentine for our fearless leader, Alan. The 2nd annual Win a Bro Date with Alan contest was magnificent! It is clear that Imgurians have a lot of love to give, and that you really like bananas and cats. To see all of the entries in their full glory, check out the Bro Date Gallery here: imgur.com/g/brodate.

And now, a poem about gaming from Alan:

your team is red
my team is blue
get ready to START
I’m going to own you

you better not camp
you better not cry
see, now you’re dead
blame it on the spy

you lost the round
don’t lose the game
now you’re dead again
next time try to aim

the game is over
you dropped the flag
better luck next time
don’t blame the lag

-Alan Schaaf, Imgur Founder & CEO

Imgur Mobile

Imgur.com now works on mobile phones. It’s even possible to upload images directly from your phone by visiting the Imgur homepage.

Aside from uploading, you can browse the gallery, create albums, look through your uploaded images, get image codes, etc. Everything you can do on the regular site, you can now do with the mobile version.

There’s no special link, all you have to do is visit imgur.com on your phone. If you’d rather use the regular version of the site, it’s easy to do that as well. Just click “Original” down at the bottom where it says “View Imgur”.

Unfortunately, uploading to web pages is not supported by the iPhone. We’d love to include that functionality for iPhone users, but it’s just not possible with the limitations Apple has set up. However, there are a couple great Imgur apps for the iPhone that support uploading, and they can be found simply by browsing the iTunes store.

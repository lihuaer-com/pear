Welcome to the Interblag

Imgur got a blog! This means you will be getting weekly updates about new features and other things that are happening with imgur. Many things will be happening with the site in the very near future, all of them good. There is a very long list of things that need to be implemented or improved on, and you will be notified on this blog after each one. Feel free to leave your feedback here too.

If you have an idea of a new feature or anything else, please post it in the forums or send me an email at alan@imgur.com. All the best things about imgur have been thought up by you guys (even the gallery), so please, don’t hesitate to share your thoughts.

The Hottest Images Online

Monday, November 1st, 2010

The new Gallery.

It’s always been a bit of a challenge for us to determine which images make it into the Imgur Gallery and which ones don’t. The Gallery is a place for funny and interesting pictures, but until now, every image was hand-picked to be in it based on a set of criteria. This was good because the images were guaranteed to be good ones, but even worse because updates were painfully slow.

The new Gallery is leaps and bounds better than the old, and will get you hooked at viewing all of the thousands of awesome images in it. Better yet, you can even submit your own captions for the images, and vote them up and down if you like or dislike them. Images that are voted up are shown at the top of the list, and images that are voted down are shown at the bottom, or may disappear all together. There may even be prizes for people who upload the highest voted image of the day. It’s also possible to vote on the captions of the image. The highest voted caption is displayed prominently under the image, and the worst captions are displayed at the very bottom.

How it works.

To get your image into the Gallery, it must be spread all over the Internet and gain a certain level of popularity. This means that you’re still able to upload images and have them remain private (just don’t share them all over the Internet!) while still being able to view the best images out there.

The “best” images are determined by you guys and the Internet community as a whole. However, we’d like to specifically thank the Twitter search engine Topsy. Topsy makes it very easy to search Twitter for the top trending Imgur images. We then take what we learn from Topsy, and look at our own data to determine if something is popular. However, nothing is perfect, so if you find an image you don’t like, just vote it down!

If you want your image to be spotlighted in the Gallery, then start sharing it on the Internet. Submit it to Reddit, tweet it on Twitter, talk about it on your blog, etc. Oh, and it would help if it was a picture of a cat.

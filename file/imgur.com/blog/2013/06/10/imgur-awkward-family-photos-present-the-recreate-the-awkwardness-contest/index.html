Imgur & Awkward Family Photos present the Recreate the Awkwardness Contest!

Your chance has come to re-enact the matching outfits, uncomfortable poses, and unexplainable images of awkward family photos. Stay true to the originals or add your own touch of cringe-worthiness.

The winner receives a Nikon D3100 14.2MP Digital SLR Camera plus a lifetime pro account, and more prizes from Imgur and Awkward Family Photos!

Here’s how it works:
Recreate one of the below Awkward Family Photos

Must be:
-An original creation made by you
-Submitted to the Recreate the Awkwardness Gallery (imgur.com/g/awkward) by 11:59PM PST on June 16, 2013.
-Adhering to the Contest Rules (below)

GRAND PRIZE:

  • Nikon D3100 14.2MP Digital SLR Camera with 18-55mm f/3.5-5.6 AF-S DX VR Nikkor Zoom Lens
  • $100 store credit to the Imgur store
  • Signed Awkward Family Photos book
  • Awkward Family Photos board game
  • Awkward Family Photos Picture Frame Kit
  • Lifetime Pro Account
  • Awkward Family Photos Christmas Puzzle
  • Your Recreation featured in the Most Viral Gallery (yes, you will keep all the reputation points you earn from this contest)

SECOND PRIZE:

  • $75 to the Imgur store
  • 2 Signed Awkward Family Photos books
  • Awkward Family Photos board game
  • Lifetime Pro Account

THIRD PRIZE:

  • $50 to the Imgur store
  • Awkward Family Photos Picture Frame Kit
  • Awkward Family Photos Christmas Puzzle
  • 1 Year Pro Account

RULES AND FINE PRINT
By entering the Recreate the Awkwardness Contest, you agree to the following Rules.

CONTEST PERIOD: Contest begins on June 10, 2013 at 12:01AM PST and ends on June 16, 2013 at 11:59PM PST.

HOW TO ENTER: NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. To enter the Contest, visit the gallery located at imgur.com/g/awkward during the contest period and upload your image directly into that gallery. Make sure you are on the Recreate the Awkwardness Gallery page while uploading. Select “add to gallery” and ensure the message “you are adding _ image(s) to the recreate the awkwardness gallery” appears. Help on uploading an image can be found here: imgur.com/help. Your entry is accepted when it appears on the Recreate the Awkwardness Gallery.

AWKWARD REQUIREMENTS:
(a) Submissions are subject to all normal gallery rules and terms of service. Imgur reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any Entrant who submits an Awkward Recreation not complying with these site wide regulations.

(b) Entries must be original, unpublished works.

(c) In the form of PNG, JPG or GIF.

(d) Only one entry per user will be accepted.

JUDGING: Each Awkward Recreation will be judged by members of the Imgur and Awkward Family Photos staff on creativity, originality, and adherence to the theme of the contest. Score and comments by users will be taken into account, but winners will be chosen solely by Imgur and Awkward Family Photos.

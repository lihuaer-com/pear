Account Stats and Profiles

I’m glad to announce a few new features for you this week: individual account statistics, and gallery profiles. Both pages can be found by hovering over your username.

Gallery Profiles

It’s now possible to go back and see the captions you’ve made as well as the images you’ve liked. Also included on this page is your gallery reputation. Gallery reputation is a gauge of how popular you are within the Imgur community. If people like the captions you’ve made and if you’ve had a few images become very popular, than chances are you’re going to have a pretty high reputation. However, if you haven’t uploaded many images and are not participating in the gallery, then your reputation is going to be much lower. Also, everyone can see this page, so stay on your best behavior!

Account Stats

The account statistics page shows you information about your account. How many images you have, how much disk space you’re using, as well as how much bandwidth you’ve used is all shown at the top. You can also find out what your most popular images, albums, and gallery captions are. This page is also private (only you can see it).

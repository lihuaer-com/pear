Sign into Imgur from Twitter, Google, Facebook and Yahoo

You can now sign in and create an Imgur account by using either your Twitter, Google, Facebook or Yahoo account. It’s safe, fast, and incredibly easy. All you have to do is select which service you’d like to associate your Imgur account with, and we do the rest.

This is possible using OpenID (for Google and Yahoo) and OAuth (for Facebook and Twitter).

“OpenID simplifies signing in. With OpenID you only have to remember one username and one password. That’s because you only give your password to your OpenID provider, and then your provider tells the websites you’re visiting that you are who you say you are. No website other than your provider ever sees your password, so you don’t have to worry about an insecure website compromising your identity.” For more information, check out openidexplained.com.

You now have no reason not to have an Imgur account. It only takes seconds to create, and you don’t even have to give us a password. If you haven’t done so already, what are you waiting for?

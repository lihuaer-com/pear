Catify Your Images!

Update: Sorry guys! April fools is over! It was fun while it lasted, but now it’s time to start thinking about next year. Sadly, this epic feature has been removed.

Here at Imgur, we’ve learned a thing or two about what makes an image popular. We’ve seen it all: babies, goats, photo bombs, Courage Wolf, etc.

But there’s always a trump card. There’s the one thing cute enough, evil enough and furry enough to make any image shoot straight to the top of our gallery. We’re talking, of course, about cats.

(Felis catus)

In an effort to help our users upload the best possible images to our site, we’re bringing you the option to automatically Catify your images! With our patented Automatic Catification technology, you have the ability to add a cat to any image you upload to Imgur.

Let’s take a look:


Just take a look at this picture here. Boring, right?


With our Automatic Catification ability, this picture is solid gold!

Let’s try it again!


Again, this picture just feels like it’s missing something.


Purr-fect!

Select the “Catify” option the next time you upload an image to Imgur, and watch the feline magic happen!

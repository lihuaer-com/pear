Subreddit Browser

Friday, April 29th, 2011

Imgur started on the social news site Reddit.com as a way for people to share their images with the community. Most imgur users are also redditors, so it’s no surprise that there are some reddit specific features built into imgur. For example, when you’re coming to the site from reddit, there will be a link back to the comments on reddit. That feature has been on the site for a while, but here’s a new one that you’re going to love:

The Subreddit Browser

You can now browse all the imgur links that are submitted to any subreddit. For example: http://imgur.com/r/pics, http://imgur.com/r/earthporn, and http://imgur.com/r/fffffffuuuuuuuuuuuu are my favorites.

Give it a try and let me know what you think! The link is imgur.com/r/AnySubreddit.

10M Uniques

Monday, April 11th, 2011

We are proud to announce that just about the time of our second birthday Imgur officially passed the 10 million unique visitors a month mark! We think that’s a cause for celebration (and anyone else looking for an excuse to drink on a Monday should join us).

Most of all we just want to say a huge “Thanks!” to our users for all the support. It’s awesome to see so many people around the world using our service, enjoying it and spreading the word to others. So, let’s raise our glasses and toast to the most entertaining images on the Internet… keep on uploading everyone!

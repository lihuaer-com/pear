Share your fun from the reddit global meetup!

Tuesday, June 21st, 2011

The Reddit Global Meetup is right around the corner, and we’re dying to see all the fun you have at it! We wanna see your rage faces, hover-hands (you know those’ll be there), Keanu Reeves sightings; everything!

We’re making an event-based gallery located at http://imgur.com/reddit/meetup2011 entirely specifically for the Reddit Global Meetup. You can submit your own images to it in one of three ways:

#1 Tweeting

Simply tweet the photo with the hashtag #redditmeetup, like so:

#2 Emailing

Email images as attachments to redditmeetup@imgur.com. This method accepts zip, rar, and plain images.

#3 Submitting

The last method you can use is a general submission form on our site. We have a link generator that allows you to easily generate a link for any image in your account. Here’s a step-by-step guide to using our link generator:

First, sign in to your account and select “Generate Image Links” from your main account screen:

Next, select any images you want to make links to and click “done”:

Copy the links you receive:

Finally, paste the links into the submission form here:
http://imgur.com/reddit/meetup2011/submit

Have a fantastic time meeting your fellow Redditors, and we can’t wait to see the stuff you come up with!

Thanks, College Volt!

Friday, June 10th, 2011

We want to say thanks to our friends at College Volt for all of their help promoting Imgur this past month. Maxx and Zac were full of really creative ideas to help us get the word out. They worked hard flyering, chalking streets and sidewalks (when it wasn’t raining), painting walls, and even thought of a cool way to project our logo at night “Batman” style! They also helped us work with 8Fest for our first ever event-based image gallery which got over 95,000 page views – really great for a concert with less than 20,000 people! Make sure to check these guys out at www.collegevolt.com.

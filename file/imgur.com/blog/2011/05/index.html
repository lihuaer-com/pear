Advanced Image Editor

Thursday, May 26th, 2011

We’re proud to announce an all new image editor, and our partnership with Aviary to make it happen.

You’re now able to easily crop and rotate your images in seconds, as well as perform powerful actions such as add text, draw, and even remove red eye and blemishes. We developed the crop and rotate tools ourselves, but the more powerful stuff was left up to Aviary and their remarkable HTML5 photo editor called Feather.

Like Aviary’s other apps, Feather is easy to use and surprisingly powerful. Instead of loading your full-fledged photo suite to do something small, it’s now easy for you to do all the typical actions right here on Imgur. There’s no need to create multiple layers, make magic wand selections, add different type of brush effects, etc. We really love it, and we hope you guys do too.

Try it out by checking “Edit” after selecting your images from the homepage. You can even edit your images after you uploaded them by clicking on the “Edit” button right underneath the image. Also, if you have an account, you can edit any image you’ve ever uploaded by by clicking the edit icon.

The guys over at Aviary have also done a nice write up about the new feature. Read it on their blog here.

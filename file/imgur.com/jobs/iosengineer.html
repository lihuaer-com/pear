iOS Engineer

Our mobile team is focused on innovation within the Imgur app, constantly improving both the technical foundation and the user interface. Have an impact building out an application that is used by hundreds of thousands, and help grow its usage into the millions.

Responsibilities

  • Take ownership of essential components in the Imgur app, impacting hundreds of thousands of users.
  • Tackle tough software problems at the core of the Imgur experience.
  • Brianstorm new ideas to continually iterate and improve the Imgur mobile experience.
  • Strive for long-term growth as a software engineer inside and outside of iOS.

Our ideal candidate will have

  • B.S. or higher in Computer science, or equivalent knowledge.
  • Be a strong software engineer.
  • Expertise in iOS development.
  • Passionate about mobile development.
  • A passion for beautiful, pragmatic code paired with a strong sense of product design.
  • A passion for the Imgur product.

How to apply

We'd love to see your work, and if you're passionate about coding or design, then we bet you have lots of things that you've done in your spare time. Along with your resume, we'd like you to send us some samples. They can be personal projects you've whipped up during a lunch break, or a full-fledged application that you've created from scratch. No matter what it is, feel free to stick it up on Github or email it along with your resume.

Email jobs@imgur.com with your resume, cover letter, at least one sample project, and your favorite image from Imgur. Please put the job you're applying for in the subject of the email.

Perk Breakdown

Standard

Pro
Total Account Images 225 Limit No Limit
Standard accounts are limited to the 225 most recent images. After that, the newest uploads will be shown and the oldest uploads will be bumped out of the account (not deleted, just hidden from within the account). There is no limit to pro accounts.
Image Compression 5 MB maximum 10 MB maximum
With standard accounts, images over 5 MB will be compressed until they are less than 5 MB. With pro accounts, your images aren't touched in any way unless they are over 10 MB, in which case they will be compressed until they reach 10MB in size.
Animated GIFs 5 MB maximum 10 MB maximum
Animated GIF images are limited to 5MB per file on standard accounts, pro accounts can upload GIFs up to 10MB each.
Thumbnails Small and Large Small, Medium, Large, Huge, Small Square, Big Square
You get 4 extra thumbnails with pro accounts, making 6 thumbnails total. The sizes are: longest side 1024px, longest side 640px, longest side 320px, longest side 160px, cropped square 160px, and cropped square 90px.
Ads Yes :-( No! :-D
When you're logged into a pro account, there are no ads anywhere to be seen. Also, if someone is browsing your albums, they won't see any ads either!
Image Analytics 5 top referrers 100 top referrers - view count and URL drill down
Keep track of where your image goes after you upload and share it. The standard account only gives you 5 out of 10 top referrers in random order, while Pro users are given the top 100 referrers. Pro accounts will also be given the ability to see how many views came from each referrer and when an image was first seen on a referrer as well.

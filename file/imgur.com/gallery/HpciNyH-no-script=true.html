I am polish concept artist and this is my painting tribute to one of the best cavalries in Europe - Polish Hussars.

The title of this piece is "Deep snow". I wanted to capture the essence of polish 16th century winter and a story of the brave knight returning from the battle... If you want to check out more of my work here's my facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/mzamecki
